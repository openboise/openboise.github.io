Open Boise

Working for an Open Source City

View My GitHub Profile

Open Boise weaves Open Source principles into the fabric of Boise city life.

The Five Characterists of an Open Source city:

  1. Fostering a culture of citizen participation
  2. Having an effective open government policy
  3. Having an effective open data initiative
  4. Promoting open source user groups and conferences
  5. Being a hub for innovation and open source business

(from The Foundation for an Open Source City by Jason Hibbets)

Projects

Community Website

Curate a community-edited resource to promote the spread and access to knowledge of the local region.

Feature encyclopedic articles, calendar events, map-based data, and tabular data sets.

Provide tools for Boise citizens to analyze, remix, and republish data made available by city and regional agencies.

Act as a platform to enable other focused-interest projects, like the Boise Architecture Project.

Facilitate citizen journalism and documentation

Act as a demonstration of Open Source principles.

Regional Crossroads

Establish and promote connections and exchange between Boise and surrounding cities to encourage the spread of people and ideas and to establish a regional identity.

Expand the community through outreach to additional populations, including children, lower SES, limited English, women, LGBTQIA, different ability, and other historically excluded groups.

Host a regional tech conference

Make employers and recruiters in other areas know about Boise tech talent

Open Policy

Work with community organizations and public officials to identify and implement policy solutions to support Open Source access to city services, allowing citizens to extend and build on top of city services and data

Promote Open Source principles as an integral and strategic part of Boise's public policy, to improve government service, increase democratic transparency, and encourage increased participation and active citizenship

How to contribute

Come say hi in #openboise on Freenode IRC

We're just getting started and identifying our objectives and scope of work. If you're passionate about some aspect of Boise and interested in promoting Open Source principles at all levels of city life, we'd love your contribution.
